Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I place an order for a sewing machine?

  1. Browse Products: Visit our website and browse through our selection of sewing machines.
  2. Product Details: Click on a sewing machine to view detailed information, specifications, and pricing.
  3. Add to Cart: Once you've decided on a sewing machine, click the "Add to Cart" button.
  4. Review Cart: Review the items in your shopping cart. You can adjust quantities or remove items if needed.
  5. Checkout: Proceed to checkout. Provide your shipping information, and choose a payment method.
  6. Payment: Enter your payment details securely to complete the purchase.
  7. Order Confirmation: After successful payment, you'll receive an order confirmation with details of your purchase.
  8. Shipping Notification: You will be notified once your sewing machine is shipped, along with tracking information if applicable.
  9. Delivery: Await the delivery of your sewing machine to your specified address.
  10. Enjoy Your Purchase: Unbox your new sewing machine, follow the setup instructions, and start sewing!

2. What kind of sewing machines you provide?

We provide all varieties of sewing machines.Mechanical, electrical, industrial are few examples of it.


For any assistance or inquiries during the process, feel free to contact our customer support team.
